COVID-19 Situation in our Stores

Before the food you buy from us reaches your plate, many steps are taken behind the scenes to ensure only the cleanest and freshest products are sold to you. At Freshco, we take your safety and that of our employees very seriously and that is why we have many protocols to make your shopping experience as safe as possible. We have partnered with distributors and different providers to make sure warehouses and delivery trucks are sanitized on a regular basis. Our stores follow rigorous measures to avoid the spread of the virus with our employees, by mandating social distancing in break areas or offices.

In front of the constantly evolving situation, our measures may be adapted in respect to health and governmental authorities. Wearing a face mask in store is mandatory. At the entrance of your store, an employee will disinfect a cart for you to simplify your shopping. Hand sanitizer is available at the entrance, next to every cash register and throughout the store alleys. As much as possible, pay with a contactless card, to avoid touching the PIN pad.

We thank you for your collaboration and wish you a truly enjoyable shopping experience.
